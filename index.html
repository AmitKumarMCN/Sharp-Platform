The Sharp Platform

An open-source social networking platform for purpose and growth-focused communities

The Sharp Platform is a scalable, secure, innovative Web 3.0 platform for member-contribution-based businesses and communities that want to introduce digital assets and digital economies to their existing users or build a new system for their new ideas and projects. Soon, businesses will require platforms that supports both Web 2.0 and Web 3.0 technologies. The Sharp Platform provides best of the both worlds with the help of Blockchain, Web 3.0, Intelligent Cloud, Mobile, and Voice-enabled technologies.

How it Works

The Sharp Platform utilizes personalized career path tools and an AI-driven Guru to empower and guide users through various steps
and milestones to achieve their self-directed goals.

The platform’s growth economy model gives users a feeling of ownership and belonging by listening to their feedback, rewarding them for their time, passion, and energy, and giving them global exposure to showcase their skills and expertise. By participating in various activities and earning tangible rewards users develop a sense of belonging and community within the platform.

Digital payments
User Sharp Tokens to buy and/or sell advertising, premium memberships, career coaching and consulting, freelancer services, exclusive content, eBooks, training, certifications, resume services, and content boosts.
Rewards
Earn rewards by interacting with the community through activities, 1-on-1’s, group contributions, etc. and spend them across the platform!
NFTs

Users can create and mint their own unique, code written NFTs that include awards, trophies, special characters, avatars, and one-of-a-kind designs. Each NFT can be bought and sold on the Sharp NFT marketplace.

Smart Contracts

A multi-protocol blockchain platform for different use cases to bring trust, transparency, and validity to the credentials and work experience for job applicants including: proof of ownership, proof of experience, proof of certification and proof of delivery and acceptance (PoDA).
